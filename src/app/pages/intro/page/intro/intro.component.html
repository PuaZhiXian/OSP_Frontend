Climate Change
What Is Climate Change?
Climate change refers to a long-term alteration in the average weather patterns that have come to define Earth's local, regional, and global climates.
Overview
Climate change is a significant global issue that is driven by anthropogenic factors such as human-induced greenhouse gas emissions, deforestation, and land-use changes. The impacts of climate change are felt worldwide, with severe consequences for ecosystems, human societies, and the global economy.
