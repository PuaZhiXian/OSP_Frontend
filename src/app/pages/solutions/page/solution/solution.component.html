Taking Action on Climate Change
The urgent need to address climate change requires bold, fast, and wide-ranging action from all sectors of society. The UN Campaign for Individual Action aims to engage citizens in the transition to a low-carbon world. To preserve a livable climate, greenhouse gas emissions must be halved by 2030 and reach net zero by 2050. This requires significant action from governments, businesses, and individuals.
